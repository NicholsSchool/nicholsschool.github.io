This Blog's Purpose

This blog, created by the students of FRC Team Electric Mayhem 4930, serves as a record of the community service, demos, and outreach accomplished by the team. We hope to share all our efforts to inspire others. In the future, we may also add robot building and programming tips. To request more info on an event or to ask a question, feel free to contact us through the information provided.
